ABOUT ME

I am a Colorado Native that loves the outdoors. I have my BA in Evolutionary and Ecology Biology, from The University of Colorado Boulder. While attending college I became the Sous Chef at SALT the Bistro, a farm-to-table restaurant that focuses on local ingredients. After graduating I moved to North Carolina for a year to work at Carolina Beverage Group as the Manager of Ingredient Quality Control. In this position I worked closely with the IT department to create a new system for filing required analysis paperwork. During my time spent with the IT department it ignited my passion for technology. This passion led me to take online coding courses and eventually moving back to Denver to attend Galvanize's Immersive Web Development program. I am a skilled Full-Stack Developer, who is currently looking open for job opportunites, and excited to share my passion for technology with others.
